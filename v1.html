Ed Galvez

AI systems for places where mistakes matter.

I have spent thirteen years putting algorithmic decision-making into production. First it was heuristic and simplex optimisation running some of the world's largest mining operations. Now it is agentic AI inside government and compliance-driven environments.

The through-line is trust. A schedule that moves real trucks and a language model that drafts real case notes fail the same way. Someone believed an output they should have questioned. Most of my work is building the structure that makes the output worth believing.

Currently I lead engineering on a government-grade case management platform in Australia, drive its AI adoption strategy under ISO 27001, and run independent research on the side.
