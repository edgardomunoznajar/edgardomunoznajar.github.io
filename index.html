What I think about

Most interesting domains are graphs pretending to be prose. A novel is a character network with a clock. A body of law is a dependency graph with jurisdiction edges. An economy is a system of equations arguing with itself. The work I enjoy is extracting that hidden structure and building tools on top of it.
